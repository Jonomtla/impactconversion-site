# Case-study summary rubric — IC site

**Surface:** First paragraph after the headline on `/case-studies/[slug]`. Also reused in the featured-card and grid-card on `/case-studies` index.
**Length budget:** 35-65 words. Long enough to set the situation, short enough to read in 8 seconds.
**Job:** Make a $5M-$20M D2C or online-ed prospect (a) recognise the situation as one they'd hire IC for, (b) feel the program described will produce numbers, (c) want to keep reading.

Pass threshold: **12/15**.

## Tiebreaker order (when two variants score equal)

1. Higher M-section score (Music is the recurring IC gap — favour the one with stronger rhythm)
2. Stronger sensory or specific noun in P1 (named platform, named metric)
3. More natural read-aloud — the one Jono would actually say in a sales call
